◆ fightTilDeath()

void Hero::fightTilDeath ( Monster &  monster)

This function is the fight mechanics.

This function makes the Hero and the Monster attack each other until one of them dies. Also, it gives the Hero xp according to its dealt damage, then gives that amount in experience point to it. After every 100 xp the Hero levels up. Which means it stats gets stronger by the multiplier bonuses.
